This application adds Burlington Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.burlingtontransit.ca and @BurlONTransit on Twitter.

Burlington Transit serves Burlington in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the City of Burlington and Burlington Transit.
https://opendata.burlington.ca/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-burlington-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with the City of Burlington and Burlington Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.burlingtontransit.ca and Twitter.
